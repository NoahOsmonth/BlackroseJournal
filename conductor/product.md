# Product Definition

## Target Audience
- Users who prefer interactive, AI-driven journaling over traditional methods.
- Individuals seeking deeper self-reflection through conversational interfaces.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Mental Well-being:** Promotes improved mental health through regular, guided reflection.
- **Personal Insight:** Helps users gain deeper understanding of their personal patterns and emotional states.
- **Safe Space:** Provides a private, non-judgmental environment for expressing thoughts at any time.
- **Emotional Tracking:** specifically enables users to track and visualize their emotional patterns over time.

## Key Features (MVP)
- **AI-Driven Reflection:** An interactive conversational interface where the AI prompts deeper reflection based on user input.
- **Mood Tracking & Analysis:** Automatic analysis of chat content to track mood and emotional trends.
- **Adaptive AI Persona:** The AI acts primarily as a supportive therapist but maintains a balanced, considerate tone—avoiding excessive flattery ("glazing") or sycophancy. It adapts its interaction style based on the user's input while remaining grounded and helpful.
- **Data Control & Personalization:** Users have full control over their data (Export/Delete) and application appearance (Light/Dark/System Theme).

## Product Goals
- To build a journal that feels like a conversation rather than a chore.
- To provide actionable insights derived from the user's own journal data.
- To ensure the user feels heard and supported without the interaction feeling artificial or overly agreeable.
